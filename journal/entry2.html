What Is A Super App

So lets begin with the concept of a Super App. A super app is an app usually on ones smartphone that is basically every app in one. Many Eastern Nations have super apps totally run & controlled by the government. From these apps one can book plane flights, reserve a dinner table, pay for items or even send money to a friend, text & call & much more. This is the reality with apps like WeChat which is the most popular app in China. With a userbase of 1.2 Billion they have a near monopoly on the Apps Market. Contrast this to United States backed Tech Companies, Facebook, Paypal, Twitter, iMessage, Signal etc. For the most part, most Americans use one or more of these apps to accomplish tasks like Sending & Receiving Messages (FB, Twitter, iMessage, Instagram, WhatsApp), or Making & Receiving Payments (Paypal, CashApp, Venmo) or even just to post to the world on your own page & talk about your day (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram). Imagine if all these apps were in one. The same way you were to write a tweet you could order food, or send someone those 5 bucks you owe them. This is the reality super apps bring & Elon has yet another crazy idea brewing.
